Small  business  supporters

Endurance Group stands for a powerful collective of global tech brands, each with its own set of customers, unique approach and offer, but united by a big ambition: to help all small business owners grow and succeed online.

Our mission

Together as a group, we’re on a mission to help millions of small business owners around the world to succeed online.

From buying a domain and setting up a website to building their brand and presence, we combine offers and expertise to give millions of entrepreneurs and small business owners all the tools they need to set up a business and grow and achieve their goals.

True companions, we’re genuinely helpful and humble folk who believe in the power of small business to drive positive change - and so we stand with them. Because as a company made of many different brands, we know that when smaller things work together, bigger things happen.

Our Leaders
Jeff Fox
CEO & President, Endurance Group

After holding multiple senior leadership roles, Jeff joined Endurance Group in August 2017 as President and Chief Executive Officer and a member of the Board of Directors. Prior to joining Endurance Group, Jeff was a principal of The Circumference Group LLC, an investment and advisory firm that he founded in 2009. Jeff recently served as President and Chief Executive Officer of Convergys Corporation and held multiple senior leadership positions at Alltel Corporation, including Chief Operating Officer, Group President - shared services, and group president - Alltel information services. His career began in investment banking working at Stephens Inc. and Merrill Lynch Pierce Fenner and Smith, following achieving a bachelor’s of science degree in economics from Duke University.

Marc Montagner
Chief Financial Officer, Endurance Group

Marc has been the Chief Financial Officer at Endurance Group since September 2015. He was previously Chief Financial Officer at LightSquared where he was the key architect of a plan of reorganization, which led to a recapitalization of the company and brought in new investors. Prior to that, Marc held roles including Executive Vice President of Strategy, Development and Distribution at LightSquared; Managing Director and Co-Head of the Global Telecom, Media and Technology Merger and Acquisition Group at Bank of America Securities; Senior Vice President, Corporate Development and M&A with the Sprint Nextel Corporation; and Managing Director in the media and telecom group at Morgan Stanley. Marc currently serves on the Board of Directors at Cogent Communications and on the audit and the corporate governance committees.

Christine Timmins Barry
Chief Operating Officer, Web Presence

Christine’s extensive experience in leading businesses and teams across the world positions her well to lead the integration of the company's portfolio of Web Presence brands - helping to create stellar experiences for our Web Presence customers and employees across the globe. Before joining Endurance Group in 2017 as its Chief Services Officer, Christine was President and Chief Officer of Windham Professionals, a revenue cycle management firm, and served as a member of Windham's board of directors. Prior to that, Christine led an organization of 80,000 employees at Convergys (now Concentrix), a global leader in customer experience outsourcing, and held Division President, Chief Sales and Chief Operations roles in various Convergys business units.

Kim Simone
Chief Operating Officer, Constant Contact

As Chief Operating Officer of Constant Contact, Kim is responsible for leading the teams focused on delivering value to Constant Contact customers through enhancements to the core email marketing product and investments in adjacent small business solutions. Kim brings years of executive and operational experience to her role as Constant Contact COO, having first joined Endurance Group in 2017 as the company’s Enterprise Transformation Officer. Prior to that, Kim served in several executive-level positions in the telecom industry at Verizon and Alltel. Her areas of responsibility have included acquisition plans and their related execution, enterprise planning and program management, application development and maintenance, capital planning, and IT infrastructure management.

John Orlando
Chief Marketing Officer, Endurance Group

A strategic and creative marketing executive with more than 20 years of experience in marketing technology, mobile entertainment and marketing services, John has been instrumental in the creation of new businesses, the establishment and enhancement of corporate brands, and the conception and launch of new products.

John has held multiple senior executive positions at public companies, including Chief Marketing Officer at Constant Contact, Chief Marketing Officer at Livewire Mobile, and Chief Marketing Officer at NMS Communications Corporation. He also previously served as President of 2ergo and as both Chairman and Vice-Chairman of the MEF in the Americas.

Mike Lillie
Chief Information Officer, Endurance Group

Mike Lillie leads the global information technology team with responsibility for infrastructure and technical operations, core data services, enterprise business systems, information security, and corporate IT services. Mike brings 25 years of information technology and leadership experience to Endurance Group, having served in several global information technology leadership roles. Prior to Endurance Group, Mike was Vice President of Information Technology for Nuance Communications, Inc., a publicly-traded leading provider of voice and language solutions, where he managed day-to-day IT operations while developing a platform to support the company’s significant revenue growth.

Curt Raffi
SVP, Website Builder Brands, Endurance Group

Curt leads a portfolio of global website builder products bringing to market website, e-commerce, domain, email, and business management tools for small businesses around the globe. Hired to lead a Product Marketing initiative at Endurance Group, Curt was later asked to serve as Vice President of Mobile Engagement and Application Development. Curt has over 20 years of online small business productization, application development, and marketing leadership expertise. Prior to Endurance Group, he led the small business payroll division for Bank of America, served as Executive Vice President at PayChoice Payroll Software, and as Managing Director of Product Marketing at Salary.com.

Mitch Haber
SVP, Global Partnerships, Endurance Group

As Head of Corporate Strategy and Partnerships, Mitch is responsible for working with the senior leadership team on the company's key strategic initiatives as well as overseeing global partnerships and corporate development. Mitch joined Endurance Group in 2000 as Director of Strategic Partnerships and has held several key positions focused on corporate development, strategic partnerships and product management. Prior to Endurance Group, Mitch was Director of Business Development for MicroAge Canada, a Fortune 500 computer reseller where he established and managed key partnerships for the company including IBM, Intel and Cisco Systems.

David Bryson
Chief Legal Officer, Endurance Group

David Bryson oversees legal and compliance affairs supporting Endurance Group. His career began at BankBoston where he amassed over 20 years in the legal department, helping to make the bank a better partner for its employees, customers, and communities. The experience David gained there – handling acquisitions, managing risk, leading teams, and finding a way to resolve complex legal challenges by providing the right advice at the right time – have proven invaluable at Endurance Group. David is also a past Chair and Board Member of ArtsBoston, and, for the last six years, was a Board Member of the Internet Infrastructure Coalition (i2C).

Tom Aurelio
Chief Human Resources Officer, Endurance Group

Tom joined Endurance Group in 2018 as Chief Human Resources Officer. Prior to that, Tom was living in Paris, where he was Executive Vice President of Human Resources for Criteo’s global business. He has 25 years of experience in human resources, primarily in the technology space, leading large, complex organizations like Priceline, General Electric, and Symantec. He is also co-founder of an innovative business solutions applications startup, SimpleCloudApps, Inc.
